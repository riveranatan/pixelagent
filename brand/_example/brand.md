# Acme Fitness

## About
- **Type**: Fitness mobile app
- **Tagline**: "Train smarter, not harder."
- **Website**: acmefitness.example.com

## Brand Concept
Acme Fitness is a modern fitness app focused on simplicity and results. The brand is clean, bold, and energetic.

## Target Audience
People who want a no-nonsense fitness app that tracks workouts and progress.

## Tone of Voice
Direct, motivational, clean. No fluff.
